About Us

Being poor and homeless does not make you any less of a human. We believe that everyone deserves a home, a bath, some stale cereal in the kitchen, a little bit of expired milk, and a whole lot of love. We gift every human being a home for a year and help them get through the pains of shitty credit scores and mild alcoholism.

We are die hard believers in positive mental health. We have 24/7 counselors who work with the “gifted” in helping them fight their fears. Each “gifted” individual is required to fill out a stack of paper work that basically allows Gifted House to do whatever we want to the “gifted” in any and all capacities .
